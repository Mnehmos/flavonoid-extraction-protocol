1. Extraction Blueprint

Polarity-Gradient Solvent Matrix

Step Solvent Volume (mL/g) Temp (°C) pH Time (min) Rationale
Defatting Hexane 10 25 7 20 Remove lipids, waxes
Crude Extraction 70% EtOH 20 40 7 30 Extract broad flavonoid range
Partition EtOAc / H2O 10 25 7 10 Enrich aglycones vs. glycosides

Flowchart

  • Crude crush → Defatting (hexane, 20 min, RT)
  • Ultrasonic extraction (70% EtOH, 40°C, 30 min) OR Soxhlet (EtOH, 2 hr)
  • Partition: EtOAc/H2O

Decision: Use ultrasonic for speed, Soxhlet for exhaustive extraction.
